# Influence-Map
This project builds a app that's influencer based


Project Objective
To develop an influencer-based interactive map that visually showcases locations, food, music, and lifestyle
activities based on influencers’ real-life experiences. The map aims to inspire users and guide them to explore local
cultures through the lens of popular online personalities, which in imitating could potentially add lifestyle-value to their
own lives!
Target Users & Their Needs
Primary Users: Individuals who follow influencers and are curious about their lifestyle.
User Needs:
-
-
-
Discover trending food spots, travel destinations, and activities endorsed by influencers.
Experience local lifestyles in a more authentic and relatable way.
Access a curated, interactive map to plan trips, outings, or find inspiration for day-to-day living
Key Deliverables
-
A functional and visually appealing (and practical) interactive map interface
-
Simple backend system to store and update location-based influencer data
-
Integration of content categories like: Restaurants & café, tourist attractions, music & entertainment
-
Filtering and search functionalities based on influencer or activity type
Special Constraints
None at this time
Challenges:
-
-
-
Structuring data from unstructured social media content
Ensuring a smooth and intuitive user interface
Balancing curated vs real-time content sourcing (if that becomes a requirement)
Expected Outcome
-
-
-
Users can easily explore and adopt influencer-endorsed lifestyles
The map becomes a go-to resource for discovering local hotspots and planning experiences based on
trusted influencer content
A more personalized, practical travel and exploration tool, blending social influence with real-world discovery
