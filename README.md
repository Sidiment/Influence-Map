# Influence Map

An influencer-based interactive map that visually showcases locations, food, music, and lifestyle activities based on influencers’ real-life experiences.

---

## Project Scope

The goal of Influence Map is to inspire users and guide them to explore local cultures through the lens of popular online personalities. By curating real-world locations endorsed by influencers, users can discover trending spots, plan trips, and find lifestyle inspiration in an engaging and interactive way.

---

## Target Users

**Primary Users:**
- Individuals who follow influencers and are curious about their lifestyle.

**User Needs:**
- Discover trending food spots, travel destinations, and activities endorsed by influencers.
- Experience local lifestyles in a more authentic and relatable way.
- Access a curated, interactive map to plan trips, outings, or find day-to-day living inspiration.

---

## Features

- 🔍 **Interactive Map:** A functional and visually appealing map interface displaying influencer-endorsed locations.
- 📂 **Backend System:** Simple backend to store and update location-based influencer data.
- 🍽️ **Content Categories:** Integration of categories such as restaurants & cafés, tourist attractions, music, and entertainment.
- 🛏️ **Filtering and Search:** Search and filter based on influencer or activity type.

**Challenges Addressed:**
- Structuring data from unstructured social media content.
- Ensuring a smooth, intuitive user interface.
- Balancing curated versus real-time content sourcing.
